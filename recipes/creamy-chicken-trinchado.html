Creamy Chicken Trinchado

Description

Creamy Chicken Trinchado is a delightful fusion of South African and Portuguese flavors, combining the spicy kick of trinchado sauce with the comfort of a creamy chicken base.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Trinchado:

  • 1.5 pounds (680g) boneless, skinless chicken breast or thigh, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons Trinchado spice mix (adjust to your preferred level of spiciness)
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped, for garnish (optional)

For the Trinchado Spice Mix:

  • 1 tablespoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon ground chili powder (adjust for spice)
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground allspice

Steps

  1. Prepare the Trinchado Spice Mix: In a small bowl, combine all the spice mix ingredients. Adjust the chili powder to your desired level of spiciness.
  2. Cook the Chicken: Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chopped chicken pieces and cook until they are browned and cooked through, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set it aside.
  3. Make the Creamy Trinchado Sauce: In the same skillet, add more oil if needed, and then add the finely chopped onion. Saut√© the onion until it's soft and translucent, about 3-4 minutes. Add the minced garlic and cook for an additional minute, or until fragrant.
  4. Spice it Up: Sprinkle the trinchado spice mix over the onion and garlic, stirring to coat the mixture evenly. Cook for a minute to release the flavors.
  5. Create the Creamy Base: Pour in the chicken broth, scraping up any browned bits from the bottom of the skillet. Add the heavy cream, salt, and black pepper. Stir well to combine.
  6. Simmer and Thicken: Let the sauce simmer for about 5-7 minutes, or until it thickens and reduces slightly.
  7. Return the Chicken: Add the cooked chicken pieces back to the skillet, coating them with the creamy trinchado sauce. Simmer for an additional 2-3 minutes until the chicken is heated through.
  8. Garnish and Serve: Garnish your Creamy Chicken Trinchado with fresh cilantro if desired. Serve it hot, ideally over cooked rice or with warm crusty bread to soak up the delicious sauce.
