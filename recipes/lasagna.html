Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna is a classic Italian dish that's always a crowd-pleaser. Here's a basic recipe for a delicious homemade lasagna. You can customize it with your favorite ingredients like different types of cheese, vegetables, or meats.

Ingredients

For the Meat Sauce:

  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 1 (15-ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Cheese Filling:

  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Additional Layers:

  • 9 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese for topping
  • Grated Parmesan cheese for topping
  • Fresh basil or parsley for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic and sauté for a few minutes until the onion is soft and translucent. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and tomato sauce. Add the dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste and adjust the seasonings as needed.
  4. In a separate bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, 1/2 cup of grated Parmesan cheese, chopped parsley, salt, and pepper.
  5. To assemble the lasagna, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
  6. Place three lasagna noodles over the sauce.
  7. Add half of the cheese mixture and spread it evenly over the noodles.
  8. Add another layer of meat sauce.
  9. Repeat the layers by adding three more noodles, the remaining cheese mixture, and more meat sauce.
  10. Place the final three noodles on top and cover with the remaining meat sauce.
  11. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese and a generous amount of grated Parmesan cheese over the top.
  12. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil, tenting it slightly to avoid touching the cheese. This will prevent the cheese from sticking to the foil.
  13. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  14. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25-30 minutes, or until the lasagna is hot and bubbly, and the cheese is golden and melted.
  15. Let the lasagna rest for about 10-15 minutes before slicing. This allows it to set, making it easier to serve.
  16. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley if desired, and enjoy!
